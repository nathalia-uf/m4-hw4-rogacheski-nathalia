Selena Gomez

About The Artist

Selena Gomez is a singer, actress, philanthropist, songwriter, and CEO of Rare Beauty. Selena was born in Grand Prairie, Texas, on July 22, 1992. She has descent from Italy and Mexico, coming from her mother, Mandy Teefey, and father, Ricardo Gomez. Selena always wanted to be in show business; her family has even shared old videos of the star performing for the camera, as shown on YouTube.

Her career started when turning nine and landing her first gig on Barney & Friends. Since then, her career has blown up, having her career expanded in all directions. A huge attribute of Selena that keeps her fans wanting more is her kind heart and authentic self, shown in all her projects and interactions.

Music Career

Throughout the years, Selena Gomez continues to shine in the music industry, evolving with her fans and relating to listeners by expressing her truth through song. Her songs have streamed over 5 billion times in the U.S.A., with 37 songs reaching the U.S. Billboard Hot 100. Selena has grown since her first appearance, singing with her pal Barney and later on various Disney Channel series. Since then, Selena has released a total of 6 pop albums. Her first three albums were under Selena Gomez & The Scene; as follows:

  • Kiss & Tell(2009)
  • A Year Without Rain(2010)
  • When the Sun Goes Down(2011)

Post her split from The Scene; she released the following three albums:

  • Stars Dance(2013)
  • Revival(2015)
  • Rare(2020)

Selena has always offered relatable and vulnerable music, showcasing herself to her fans and audience. She continues to evolve and diversify her music by releasing EPs, singles, and featuring in songs. Because of her father's Mexican descent, Selena grew up speaking Spanish as her second language leading to her 2021 EP release, Revelacion, entirely in Spanish. She continues to collaborate with multiple Latin artists, and the latest feature is Calm Down by Rema, a Nigerian musician.

Selena has built an immense following worldwide. Her fans support all her new endeavors. All of them are eagerly awaiting new music from the artist. Discover more about Selena Gomez's music here.

Career Outside of Music

Selena Gomez has stood out in the music industry and successfully became a well-known actress, philanthropist, and business owner.

Acting

Her first show appearance was on Barney & Friends, but most remember her from her iconic lead role on the Disney Channels, Wizards of Waverly Place, as Alex Russo from 2007-2012. She has also starred in various movies, successfully taking on multiple roles, winning the Kid's Choice Awards for Favorite T.V. Actress from 2009-2013. She was behind the scenes as an executive producer of the Netflix Original Series, 13 Reasons Why. Her latest appearance on T.V. was on the Hulu Orginal Series, Only Murders in the Building.

Philanthorpy

Selena Gomez works with multiple organizations that address poverty, inequality, hunger, mental health, and many other causes. She puts in the work, physically and with her influence. Selena has been a UNICEF ambassador for over 12 years, raising money to help those in need. She stands alongside inequality movements, such as Black Lives Matter and When We All Vote.

Most recently, Selena has started her charity, Rare Impact Fund, to bring awareness to mental health. She has been open about her personal struggle and how balance is not easy, but it's an area she works on every day, and she hopes the fund facilitates others on the same journey. The fund aims to raise $100 million within ten years to provide mental health services while continuously offering a positive space and information for all on the Rare Impact Blog.

C.E.O of Rare Beauty

Much of Selena's recent active pulls from her latest album, Rare. She has built her brand on this one word, bringing forward her Rare Impact Fund and her famous Rare Beauty line.

Selena Gomez found Rare Beauty in 2020. The beauty line offers makeup that embraces the user's natural beauty and what makes them unique. Its mission is to promote self-acceptance and provide a community that does not discriminate against others. In its short existence, the new competitor has several makeup product launches that have become cult favorites of the makeup community. The bonus with each product is that every purchase is working for a better tomorrow, cultivating individuals eager to purchase and join the mission.
